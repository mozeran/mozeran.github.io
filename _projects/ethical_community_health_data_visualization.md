For my project, I am interested in researching and developing a set of criteria and/or guidelines for the creation of data visualization in relation to community health data. The focus of these guidelines would be to establish methods for creating community health data visualization and to communicate the nuance and ethical implications of revealing disparities among diverse communities. The purpose of the guidelines would be to establish the responsibility a researcher takes on as a visual storyteller, especially concerning the decisions made when manipulating health data.
